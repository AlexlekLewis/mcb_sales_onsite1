Tuscany Blinds
TUSCANY has several features that provide competitive advantage over other PVC based systems sold by 
competitors in Australia. Tuscany has been specially developed to better handle typical Australian 
conditions, through its unique high temperature chemical formulation and co-extrusion production 
technology.  Rated up to 55°C, Tuscany will outperform almost all other PVC products sold in Australia.  
While Tuscany is a high-quality product, like any plastic slat product, it can have some limitations in 
extreme conditions.  It is not recommended that very dark colours be installed into windows that receive full 
sun and where the temperature exceeds the Tuscany rating of 55°C which does happen, especially for 
North facing windows. Tuscany offers a contemporary color palette with some 50mm slat available in 
smooth and embossed and 63mm slat in smooth only.
1. Please deduct 10mm off the width and 15mm off the drop when ordering a “Recess” fit blind. Unless you 
specify that “NAM (No allowance made)”, we will make all blinds to sizes you specify. 
2. A maximum width of 2700mm for Slat, Bottom rail & Fascia 
3. Minimum Blind size is 180mm. Blinds under 400mm carry a surcharge as per pricing table. These 
blinds are made by hand. These are not supplied with a warrantee. Please also be aware that blinds 
under 250mm will not have lift cord, these are tilt only. 
4. Please advise if Face or Recess on the order form. This is for bracket purpose only. 
5. Minimum cord length is 500mm 
6. For Butting blinds, please deduct 70mm off the butt blind for face and 80mm for recess. 
7. Box Brackets come standard with blinds under 250mm in width otherwise they are $7.00 pair and centre 
support brackets are $2.20 each.  Hold Down clips only come standard with blinds under 400mm in 
width otherwise they are $7.00 pair. 
8. Common Headrails are not available but common fascia can cover two or more blinds up to 3000mm. 
9. Also, please note:  Cloth tapes have been discontinued except for white 50mm, however we have some 
leftover stock for your Tuscany Blinds as illustrated below: Cloth Tape has surcharge of 20% on the 
blind price.
