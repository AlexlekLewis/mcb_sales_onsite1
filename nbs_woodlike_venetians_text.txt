Urbanwood Blinds
Urbanwood is an alternative wood product that looks and feels like real wood. Urbanwood is a polystyrene 
product that resists fading, cracking, discolouration and warping.  It has a smooth finish on the slat.  
Urbanwood is completely moisture proof, so it is suitable for humid and wet areas such as kitchens and 
bathrooms where wood blinds are not recommended.  The colour is always consistent, so the customer will 
always receive a blind that is the same colour as the sample they chose from.  Urbanwood is also 
washable.  
Urbanwood has a curved 83mm Valance which is made from PVC foam because co-extruded polystyrene 
does not work so well for complex shapes in thicker material.  The bottom rail is also made from PVC foam. 
Both the Valance and Bottom Rail are painted and printed with the same design as the slat so they match 
perfectly.  All the slats have undergone stringent heat testing and have achieved the following results; no 
distortion present between 48° and 60° and 1.5mm maximum movement between 61° and 71°. 
1. Please deduct 10mm off the width and 15mm off the drop when ordering a “Recess” fit blind. Unless you 
specify that “NAM (No allowance made)”, we will make all blinds to sizes you specify. 
2. A maximum width of 2700mm with Warrantee for Slat, Bottom rail & Fascia 
3. Minimum Blind size is 180mm. Blinds under 400mm carry a surcharge as per pricing table. These 
blinds are made by hand. These are not supplied with a warrantee. Please also be aware that blinds 
under 250mm will not have lift cord, these are tilt only. 
4. Please advise if Face or Recess on the order form. This is for bracket purpose. 
5. Minimum cord length is 500mm 
6. For Butting blinds, please deduct 70mm off the butt blind for face and 80mm for recess. 
7. Box Brackets come standard with blinds under 250mm in width otherwise they are $7.00 pair and centre 
support brackets are $2.20 each.  Hold Down clips come standard with blinds under 400mm in width 
otherwise they are $7.00 pair. 
8. Common Headrails are not available but common fascia can cover two or more blinds up to 2800mm. 
9. Also, please note:  Cloth tapes have been discontinued except for white 50mm, however we have some 
leftover stock for your Urbanwood Blinds as illustrated below: Cloth Tape has surcharge of 20% on the 
blind price.
